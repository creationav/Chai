about us

What makes our coffee special?

Chai, also known as "Masala Chai," is a flavorful and aromatic spiced tea that originated in India and is now enjoyed worldwide. It is typically made by boiling black tea leaves with a combination of water, milk, sugar (or sweetener), and a blend of warming spices.

Chai is rich, creamy, sweet, and spicy, offering a comforting and invigorating taste. The spices vary depending on regional or personal preferences, making every cup unique.

In India, chai is more than a beverage; it is a symbol of hospitality and community. It is often served to guests, at roadside stalls ("chai wallahs"), and during social gatherings.
